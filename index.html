About Us

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

We are a student community.
This community was founded to address a growing problem in students that is lack of practical knowledge and lack of guidance.

So the mission of our community is to solve these two major problems. We will help ambitious students who lack technical knowledge and support to learn and grow. Currently we are doing this by providing web and android development services at minimum cost.
